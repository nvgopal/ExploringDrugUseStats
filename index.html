Exploring Drug Use Statistics

Whatever we may think about it, positive or negative, drug usage in its numerous forms is a deeply integrated activity in many social settings and individual lives throughout the world.

People have tried and failed to exponge the issue as a whole, pointing us in the clear direction that rather than fight it's existence, we must manage it's impacts. However, before we take such action, we must gather our information more completely.

Here, we present some statistical graphics to help us better understand the issue we intend to address.














In this image we learn that unfortunately, drug deaths are in issue throughout the world. People have overdone their drug usage beyond what is responsible, reasonable, or safe.











This graphic shows us how trends of drug use across major countries have performed from 1990 to 2017. We can see a clear rise in usage, including America. This is a sign that programs and policies should be in place to account for this growing population.



















Taking a deeper look into how America has been impacted by an overall rise in drug usage, this map shows us in what states drug usage is the most popular. As some might expect, we see the highest number of drug users in states with the highest populations.


















Now that drug usage as a whole is established, we need to figure out what substances specifically are being used.
